About me

Hello, I’m Eugene Petrenko, or Evgeny Petrenko, or Евгений Петренко. Follow @jonnyzzz

Bio

Eugene leads the IDE Services department at JetBrains, steering a suite of products that helps optimize development teams’ productivity at company scale. With a Ph.D. in computer science, he merges his deep technical expertise with leadership, overseeing strategic collaboration and technical architecture. A speaker and blogger, Eugene continues to influence the tech sphere with innovative ideas and practical solutions.

Eugene was an IntelliJ IDEA software developer at JetBrains, speaker, trainer, blogger. His interests include software development, distributed systems, infrastructure, cloud, programming languages, team work. Eugene holds a Ph.D. in computer science. He writes code in Java, Kotlin, Go, Terraform, C/C++, JavaScript, or any other language that is needed to achieve his goals.

A Kotlin fan for years, he started using Kotlin years before the 1.0 release and completed his first production Kotlin app back in 2013. Eugene has made many talks and projects on Kotlin.

Eugene works in the IT field for more than 23 years. At JetBrains since 2004. Taking part in many awesome things including ReSharper, TeamCity, Upsource, Toolbox App, Space, IntelliJ IDEA, the IntelliJ IDEA Platform, and few more yet internal projects.

You may find several photos of me here and there. There avatars are there and here for conference sites.
